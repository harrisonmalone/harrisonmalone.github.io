The xkey situation is an interesting one. On the one hand it’s a beautiful product that I think I’d get a lot of enjoyment out of but how much would I actually use it. Would it be away in the cupboard on weeks where I’m busy at work or if I have a podcast to record or a big running week.

There’s also the fact that I'm keen on learning specifically piano and there’s a perfectly good piano in the house already that just needs to be tuned up a bit. This is what makes my decision; I think I should put a hold on getting the xkey until I practice on the big piano for a while.

The priority in terms of products to buy should be a new Mac. This is the most important item in my possession and it needs to be updated at some stage this year. You could even invest a bit of money depending on some guidance I get from family.

In terms of learning piano, get the thing tuned at some stage as it's quite dodgy as it stands. You should also try out some of the piano apps like Simply Piano and flowkey.
