Reading 2019 Edition

13 April 2019

I've had a think about how I'm reading and I've used Blogtrotter less and less over the past few months. So I've decided to remove my subscription and instead have this page as a dictionary of my favorite bloggers and where to find them.

So without further ado:

  • Daring Fireball
    • The best location for Apple news. Gruber also posts lots of other interesting articles about the internet and culture.
  • M.G. Siegler
    • The best writer on Medium and some really interesting takes on Apple and tech in general.
  • Macstories
    • Some nice iOS focussed stories from Federico Viticci. Particularly enjoy his iPad diaries.
  • 512 Pixels
    • Some more nice Apple related writing.
  • Six Colors
    • Mac reviews and general Apple talk.
  • Hacker Newsletter
    • A collection of the best tech news and random interesting topics on a weekly basis.
