SF

December 7 2018

Just a quick little post late on a hot Friday night in December. I'm kind of bored and was just reminiscing on some memories by looking at photos from last years trip to the west coast of the United States.

I started off by hiring out a bike and riding it to the golden gate bridge. This is on the city side and this is up the top of a gigantic hill that we were crazy to ride up.

The painted ladies are incredibly photographic buildings.

This is kind of an underrated photo in my collection. There's some nice colour in the bushes and I'm overlooking the bay from a nice spot. Twin peaks was an incredible spot to take photos from. I went up there twice.

Alcatraz is obviously a key thing to get a photo of. There's also this photo of it in the background overlooking the bay again.

The pacific ocean made me feel closer to home than I had been for a year. The beach is very different there.

The sun set on what was one of my favorite city experiences I'd ever had.
