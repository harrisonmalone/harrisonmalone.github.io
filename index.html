So what do you do?

I make web apps and software. I'm still very much a developer in training — currently at Le Wagon — but everyday I learn new skills and get better. I jumped into this industry as I want to master something technical, something more than just being able to write and research. I love making stuff; fitting together the Lego pieces to build great products that improve humanity. I see this opportunity in tech more than anywhere else.

I'm currently looking for work as a Ruby and Rails programmer.

I write columns about tech. I enjoy thinking about how technology is shaping my life; from product recommendations to app addictions I have. I've been writing about all sorts of things full time for about 2 years now.

I curate a sports podcast called The Melon with one of my best friends Nick Boylan. I've always been fascinated by sports broadcasting (I used to commentate myself playing in the backyard) and that continues today through this little project.
