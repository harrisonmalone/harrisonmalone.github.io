Kids

November 11 2018

I love looking at photos when me and Jake were kids. It's kind of relaxing and it makes me very nostalgic. I used the Google photo scanner to scan these photos and then uploaded them to dropbox.

This is when Ruby was a pup. I love looking at the old Stradbroke Avenue especially the colour of the couch and the VCRs in the background. There is also this photo of the house pre renovation with the old floors and my aunties dogs inside, which was a rarity at that time (dogs being inside).

Speaking of cute photo's of Jake when he was a baby there is an abundance, like the one of him eating a chicken bone or wearing his favorite orange Kathmandu jumper.

There are two photo's that I love of the days that me and Jake were born. No idea who took them but they did a great job. The first is me with my proud parents, also with my aunty and pop hovering the background with their very 90's clothes on. Then there is a family shot of the day Jake was born. This is perhaps my all time favorite childhood photo and one of the only photos with all of us together as a family.

A big bulk of photos were taken on Jake's first birthday whilst we still lived in Mentone such as this, this and this one. The latter of which is a great one becuase it really captures the spirit of my pop.

As a kid I was lucky enough to go on a few great holidays. When I was around 6, my extended family (cousins) went on a trip to the outback, driving to Adelaide then through the Flinders Ranges and right up to Cameron's Corner (border of NSW, QLD and SA). This photo sitting on the front of a 4-wheel-drive is a classic and shows how little we all were. Don't know how our parents managed to stay sane with 5 toddlers going crazy. There is also a nice family shot on the same holiday.

I can vaguely remember the first time I saw snow and went skiing down a mountain at dangerous speeds. I especially love Jake's face in it.

A few more cute photo's that I'll chuck into this post are me and Jake wearing raincoats and pulling funny faces, a shot with me wearing a Vampires footy jumper, me and Jake about to go to a music concert in our best corduroy jackets and a classic photo of us on the PlayStation 2 in matching pajamas.

There's way more photo's of us as kids that I'll keep incrementally putting up in the future. At least for now I'm happy with the structure of these posts.
